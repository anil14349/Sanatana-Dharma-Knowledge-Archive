**Last Updated:** June 2026

# Purpose

The Research Backlog is SDKA's prioritized research pipeline. Every discovered topic enters here immediately. Nothing is forgotten.

For prioritization criteria, see `docs/05_Editorial_System.md`. For backlog strategy, see `02_Master_Blueprint_v4.0.md` §32.

---

# Priority Scale

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| P1 | Research now — high value, ready for production |
| P2 | Research soon — strong value, needs scheduling |
| P3 | Research later — valuable but not urgent |
| P4 | Exploratory — needs scoping before research |

---

# Status Values

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `backlog` | Identified, not started |
| `research` | Active research in progress |
| `review` | KO in review |
| `published` | KO published; content may be in production |
| `deferred` | Paused — note reason |
