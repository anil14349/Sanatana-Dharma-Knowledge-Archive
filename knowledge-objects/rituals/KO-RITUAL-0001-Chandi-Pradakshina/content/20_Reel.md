# Reel Script
**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

## Hook
Why do devotees stop halfway around many Shiva temples?

## Body
Many traditional Shaiva temples guide devotees along a different circumambulation path.

The reason isn't superstition.

It's temple architecture.

The Soma Sutra carries Abhisheka water from the sanctum and, in many traditions, devotees avoid crossing it.

Nearby, the shrine of Chandikeshvara often marks an important turning point.

Practices vary by Agama and by temple, so always follow local guidance.

## Closing
The next time you visit a Shiva temple, don't just see the deity.

Let the architecture teach you.
