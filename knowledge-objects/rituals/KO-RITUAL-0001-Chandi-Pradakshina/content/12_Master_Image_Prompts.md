# Global Visual Identity

## Style

- Archaeological museum exhibit
- Temple guidebook illustration
- National Geographic documentary aesthetic
- Historically accurate South Indian Shaiva temple architecture
- Premium editorial quality

## Colour Palette

- Granite grey
- Warm sandstone
- Muted saffron
- Aged bronze
- Soft golden sunrise
- Deep earthy browns

## Camera

- 24mm or 35mm lens
- Eye-level perspective
- Symmetrical framing where possible
- Cinematic composition

## Quality

- Ultra detailed
- Realistic stone textures
- Authentic Chola/Vijayanagara detailing
- Clean composition
- High readability

## Negative Prompt (Use for every image)

No anime, cartoon, fantasy armor, glowing magic, modern clothing, modern buildings, neon colours, sci-fi elements, plastic textures, CGI look, watermark, logo, text, extra limbs, distorted anatomy, blur, low resolution.
