# Slide 3 — The Hidden Clue

The answer isn't written on a signboard.

It's built into the temple itself.

## Soma Sutra

The sacred channel carrying Abhisheka water from the sanctum.

In many Agamic traditions, devotees avoid stepping across it out of reverence.

*Practices vary by temple and tradition.*

# Caption

**Why is Pradakshina different in many Shiva temples?**

Many devotees notice the change in the path but never learn the reason.

Our research found that in many traditional Shaiva temples, the route of circumambulation is closely connected to temple architecture, Agamic traditions, and the location of the Soma Sutra and Chandikeshvara shrine.

Practices are **not identical across all Shiva temples**. They vary according to Agama, regional tradition, and individual temple customs. Always follow the guidance provided by the temple you visit.
