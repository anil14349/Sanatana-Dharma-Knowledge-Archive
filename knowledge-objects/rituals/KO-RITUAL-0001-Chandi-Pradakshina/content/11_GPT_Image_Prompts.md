## Global Style
Create premium editorial illustrations inspired by South Indian temple architecture. The style should resemble a museum guidebook mixed with National Geographic documentary photography. Use historically accurate Chola/Vijayanagara granite architecture, muted earth tones, warm sunrise lighting, realistic stone textures, and clean compositions. Never include text, logos, or watermarks.

## Negative Prompt (Apply to every image)
Anime, fantasy, CGI, neon colors, modern clothing, modern buildings, sci-fi, glowing magic, watermark, logo, text, blur, low quality, extra limbs.
