# Content Strategy
**KO:** KO-RITUAL-0001 — Chandi Pradakshina

## Purpose
Define the publishing strategy for all derivative content.

## Primary Goal
Educate devotees about the unique circumambulation followed in many traditional Shaiva temples while avoiding unsupported universal claims.

## Target Audience
- Temple visitors
- Sanatana Dharma learners
- Architecture enthusiasts
- Youth discovering temple traditions

## Core Message
Temple architecture itself teaches ritual.

## Key Takeaways
1. Chandi Pradakshina is associated with many Shaiva traditions.
2. Soma Sutra is central to understanding the path.
3. Chandikeshvara marks an important ritual point.
4. Practices vary by Agama and temple.
5. Always follow local temple guidance.
