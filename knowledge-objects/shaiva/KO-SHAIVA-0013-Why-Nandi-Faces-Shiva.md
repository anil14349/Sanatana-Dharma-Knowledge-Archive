## Summary
Nandi is far more than Shiva's vehicle. In Shaiva temple tradition, Nandi is the foremost devotee, guardian of the sanctum, and chief of Shiva's ganas. His position directly facing the Shiva Linga is prescribed primarily by the Shaiva Agamas and reinforced by temple tradition.
