# CLAUDE.md — Sanatana Dharma Knowledge Archive (SDKA)

## Project Overview

SDKA v4.0 is a documentation-only knowledge archive (no code, no build system). It preserves and organizes authentic Sanatana Dharma / Indic Knowledge Systems content for multi-platform publishing (Instagram, YouTube, website, encyclopedia, AI assistant, books, courses).

Guiding principle: **Research First → Understanding Second → Storytelling Third → Virality Last**

## Structure

- Root: vision, blueprint, research manual, changelog, research backlog
- `/docs/` — detailed architecture docs (vision, knowledge objects, research methodology, editorial, visual system, knowledge graph, content production, roadmap, AI architecture, style guide)
- `/knowledge/` — knowledge objects organized by domain (currently has `/graph/` subfolder)
- `/templates/` — templates for articles, carousels, knowledge objects, research cards, YouTube scripts

## Conventions

- All content is Markdown
- Knowledge objects follow the schema in `docs/03_Knowledge_Object_Specification.md`
- Research follows `03_Research_Manual_v2.md` methodology
- Style follows `docs/11_Style_Guide.md`
- Version history tracked in `CHANGELOG.md`

## When Editing Content

- Preserve Sanskrit diacritics (ā, ī, ū, ṛ, ṣ, ś, ṇ, ṭ, ḍ, ñ)
- Use IAST transliteration standard
- Cite primary sources (scripture references) before secondary sources
- Maintain the research-first approach — claims need textual evidence
