# Purpose

The SDKA Research Maturity Model (RMM) defines the lifecycle of every research topic from its initial idea to a continuously maintained reference. It provides objective entry and exit criteria so every Knowledge Object follows the same quality standards.

---

# Overview

| Level | Name | Goal |
|------|------|------|
| R0 | Idea | Topic identified |
| R1 | Preliminary Research | Basic understanding established |
| R2 | Evidence Validated | Sources evaluated and evidence classified |
| R3 | Repository Ready | Knowledge Package completed and approved |
| R4 | Published | Content derived and published |
| R5 | Living Document | Updated as new evidence emerges |

---

# R0 – Idea

## Entry Criteria

- Topic proposed.
- Initial research value identified.

## Deliverables

- Working title.
- Research questions.
- Priority assessment.

## Exit Criteria

- Approved for preliminary research.

---

# R1 – Preliminary Research

## Objectives

Build an initial understanding of the topic.

## Deliverables

- Research notes.
- Candidate primary sources.
- Preliminary bibliography.
- Known misconceptions.
- Open questions.

## Exit Criteria

- Sufficient evidence collected for validation.

---

# R2 – Evidence Validated

## Objectives

Verify claims and classify evidence.

## Required Deliverables

- Primary source review.
- Secondary source comparison.
- Evidence Matrix.
- Confidence ratings.
- Regional variation notes.
- Living tradition assessment.

## Exit Criteria

- Major claims supported or explicitly marked unresolved.

---

# R3 – Repository Ready

## Objectives

Create a complete SDKA Knowledge Package.

## Required Package

- README.md
- metadata.yaml
- primary-sources.md
- evidence-matrix.md
- bibliography.md
- knowledge-graph.md
- CHANGELOG.md
- Additional topic-specific files

## Quality Gates

- Evidence Audit Checklist passed.
- Repository review completed.
- Metadata validated.
- Cross-links established.

## Exit Criteria

- Status: Frozen
- Approved for content production.

---

# R4 – Published

## Objectives

Transform the Knowledge Object into public-facing educational content.

## Deliverables

- Instagram carousel
- Reel / Shorts script
- Website article
- YouTube outline (where applicable)

## Requirements

- Every claim traceable to the Knowledge Package.
- No unsupported simplifications.

---

# R5 – Living Document

## Objectives

Maintain long-term accuracy.

## Triggers for Update

- New primary source discovered.
- New scholarly publication.
- Field verification completed.
- Archaeological findings.
- Editorial corrections.

## Deliverables

- Updated documentation.
- Revised Evidence Matrix.
- CHANGELOG entry.
- Version increment.

---

# Promotion Rules

| From | To | Requirement |
|------|----|-------------|
| R0 | R1 | Research approved |
| R1 | R2 | Evidence collected |
| R2 | R3 | Knowledge Package + Audit completed |
| R3 | R4 | Content published |
| R4 | R5 | Ongoing maintenance begins |

No level may be skipped.

---

# Responsibilities

## Researcher

- Collect evidence.
- Document uncertainty.
- Record sources.

## Reviewer

- Validate evidence.
- Review classifications.
- Confirm quality gate.

## Publisher

- Produce content from approved Knowledge Objects only.

---

# Success Metrics

A mature Knowledge Object should:

- Cite reliable sources.
- Distinguish evidence from interpretation.
- Document regional diversity.
- Record misconceptions.
- Link into the Knowledge Graph.
- Be maintainable through version history.
